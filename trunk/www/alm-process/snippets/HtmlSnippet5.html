AnkhSVN started as Arild Fines' final year development project at the Engineering Faculty of the Oslo College in Norway. Karl Fogel (one of the Subversion “originals”) was Arild’s outside mentor. Arild continued the project while pursuing a master's degree in Informatics at the University of Oslo. Nowadays, AnkhSVN is a true open source project under an Apache-style license. Arild continues to leads the project and multiple committers develop the code. CollabNet hosts AnkhSVN, thus helping to grow the AnkhSVN community and the company contributes code to the project.
